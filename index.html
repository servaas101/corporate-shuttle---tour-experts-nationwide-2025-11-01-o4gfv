About Pogiso Tours

Decades of Excellence in Corporate Transportation

Our Story

Founded over two decades ago, Pogiso Tours has grown from a small local shuttle service to one of the nation's most trusted corporate transportation providers. Starting with just a handful of vehicles, we built our reputation on reliability, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Today, our extensive fleet serves thousands of corporate clients nationwide, from Fortune 500 companies to innovative startups, all trusting us with their most important transportation needs.

Our Mission & Vision

At Pogiso Tours, our mission is to redefine corporate transportation by delivering safe, punctual, and professionally executed shuttle services that exceed expectations. We believe that every journey matters, and we're committed to making each one exceptional. Our vision is to become the nation's premier corporate shuttle provider by continuously innovating our services, investing in our people and fleet, and maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism. We're dedicated to building long-term partnerships with our clients through transparency, reliability, and a genuine commitment to their success.

Booking with Pogiso Tours is simple and convenient. You can visit our website at www.pogisostours.co.za/booking/ to reserve your shuttle online, or call our 24/7 call centre at info@pogisostours.co.za for personalized assistance. We offer flexible booking options for one-time trips, recurring services, and long-term contracts. Our team will work with you to customize a solution that meets your specific corporate transportation needs and budget requirements.

We understand that plans can change. Cancellations made 24 hours in advance receive a full refund with no penalties. Modifications to pickup times, locations, or vehicle types can be made up to 4 hours before your scheduled service at no additional charge. For cancellations within 24 hours, a 50% service fee applies. Emergency situations are handled on a case-by-case basis – we encourage you to contact our 24/7 call centre to discuss your specific circumstances.
